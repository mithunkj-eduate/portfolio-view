About

Designing spaces that inspire generations

FAQ

Common Questions

A typical residential project takes 12–24 months from concept to completion. This includes 2–3 months of design and drawing preparation, 1–2 months for regulatory approvals, and 9–18 months of construction depending on scale and complexity. We provide a detailed project schedule at the inception stage so you always know what's happening next.
Absolutely. While based in Bangalore, we regularly work across South India — Coorg, Mysuru, Chennai, Hyderabad, and Goa are among our most frequent project locations. We've also completed projects in Delhi NCR, Mumbai, and internationally in Singapore and UAE. Travel and site visit costs are discussed transparently upfront.
Fees are typically structured as a percentage of project construction cost, ranging from 6–12% depending on project complexity and scope. For masterplanning and large commercial projects, we work on retainer-plus-milestone models. All fee structures are transparent, documented in our appointment letter, and discussed in the initial consultation at no charge.
Yes. Our team has extensive experience navigating BBMP, BDA, BMRDA, and various municipal body approvals across Karnataka and other states. We handle building plan sanctions, commencement certificates, occupancy certificates, and specialist NoCs (fire, aviation, environment). This is included in our full-service offering.
Sustainability isn't an add-on — it's baked into our design process from day one. Every project defaults to passive cooling strategies, natural ventilation, rainwater harvesting, and high-performance glazing. For clients seeking formal certification, we guide through LEED, GRIHA, and IGBC pathways. Sustainable design also reduces lifecycle costs significantly.
